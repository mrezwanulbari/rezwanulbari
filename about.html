About Me

Cybersecurity & Cloud Security Leader | Enterprise Architecture | Threat Intelligence

Professional Journey

I am a cybersecurity and cloud security leader with 17+ years of progressive experience designing and delivering enterprise security programs across multi-branch banking and high-availability cloud environments. My career has been defined by a commitment to translating complex security challenges into actionable, measurable solutions that strengthen organizational resilience.

Throughout my career, I have led security initiatives across diverse domains including threat detection and response, vulnerability management, compliance frameworks, zero trust architecture, and cloud security posture management. My approach combines technical depth with strategic thinking, ensuring that security investments directly support business objectives while maintaining the highest standards of operational excellence.

Core Philosophy

I believe that effective cybersecurity is built on three pillars: visibility (knowing what's happening in your environment), automation (responding at machine speed), and continuous improvement (learning from every incident and threat). This philosophy has guided my work in implementing advanced SIEM platforms, orchestrating incident response through SOAR, and building security cultures that prioritize both protection and productivity.
