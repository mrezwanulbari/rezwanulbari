Frequently Asked Questions

What types of projects do you work on?

I work on a wide range of cybersecurity projects including enterprise security architecture, threat detection and response, vulnerability management, compliance implementation, cloud security, and zero trust architecture design. Each engagement is tailored to the organization's specific needs and maturity level.

What is your consulting approach?

I follow a comprehensive approach that starts with understanding your current security posture, identifying gaps and risks, developing a strategic roadmap, and providing hands-on implementation support. I believe in knowledge transfer and building internal capabilities.

Do you offer remote consulting?

Yes, I offer both remote and on-site consulting engagements. Remote engagements are conducted through secure video conferencing, screen sharing, and collaborative tools. I'm flexible and can adapt to your preferred working arrangement.

What industries have you worked with?

I have extensive experience in financial services (banking), technology, healthcare, and government sectors. My expertise spans both large enterprises and mid-sized organizations, with experience in multi-country operations.

How do you stay current with security trends?

I maintain continuous learning through industry certifications, attending security conferences, participating in threat intelligence communities, and actively researching emerging threats and technologies. This ensures my recommendations are always aligned with current best practices.

What is your response time for inquiries?

I aim to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours. For urgent matters, please mention the urgency in your message and I'll prioritize accordingly. You can also reach out via phone for time-sensitive discussions.

Do you speak at conferences or events?

Yes, I'm available for speaking engagements at security conferences, industry events, and corporate training sessions. Topics include threat detection, zero trust architecture, SOAR automation, cloud security, and enterprise security best practices.

Can you help with compliance audits?

Absolutely. I have extensive experience with compliance frameworks including PCI-DSS, ISO 27001, NIST, CMMC, and SOX. I can help with gap analysis, control implementation, audit preparation, and ongoing compliance management.
